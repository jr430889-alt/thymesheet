================================================================================

All of your data is stored exclusively on YOUR computer:

- Application settings: Stored locally on your computer

Your Data Belongs to You:

- We cannot view, access, or recover your data

Your Responsibility:

4. INFORMATION WE DO COLLECT
================================================================================

4.1 License Activation Data
When you activate the Software, we collect minimal hardware identifiers to
bind your license to your computer:

What We Collect:
- Unique hardware identifiers derived from your computer
- These identifiers are used solely for license validation
- No personally identifiable information is collected

How It's Used:
- To ensure your license is used on only one computer as per the license terms
- To allow the Software to reactivate automatically if you reinstall

Where It's Stored:
- Stored locally on your computer only
- Not transmitted to any external servers

4.2 Update Checking (Optional)
If you choose to check for updates using the built-in update checker:

What Happens:
- The Software connects to external servers to check if a newer version exists
- No personal information is transmitted during this check
- No time tracking data is transmitted
- No usage data or analytics are sent

What May Be Collected by Third Parties:
- Your IP address may be visible to the update server (standard web request)
- Connection timestamp (standard server logs)
- This information is NOT collected by us, but by the server infrastructure
  used for software distribution

How to Opt Out:
- You can disable update checking by blocking the Software's network access
  using your firewall
- The Software will continue to function normally offline

- Your data is stored on your computer using standard file system security
